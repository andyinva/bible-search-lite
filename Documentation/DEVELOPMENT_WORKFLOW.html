What Users Download

When you create a release, users download:

  • Application files (bible_search_lite.py, etc.)
  • Database (bible_data.sql.gz)
  • Installer (setup.py or setup_win11.py)
  • Documentation (README.md)

They do NOT get:

  • dev_manager.py
  • DEV_MANAGER_GUIDE.md
  • Your .git folder
